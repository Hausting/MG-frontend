ROADMAP

  • 1

    $GOAT Token

    The first utility in the project will be the $GOAT ERC20 Token. This token will be earned by staking your Mutant Goats NFTs and will enable you to purchase/claim exclusive merch, airdrops, whitelist spots for big projects, and so much more. It can also be traded for ETH which would count as a passive income opportunity.

  • 2

    Marketplace

    A marketplace will be available where holders of Mutant Goats can buy or claim items that go from merch and cheap whitelist spots to IRL Crypto Conference passes, using the $GOAT token.

  • 3

    Yield Farming

    • Stake your Mutant Goats NFTs and earn daily $GOAT rewards.
    • 1/1 holders earn 1000 $GOAT per day.
    • Rare Goats holders earn 500 $GOAT per day.
    • Common Goats holders earn 100 $GOAT per day.
  • 4

    Governance

    While Mutant Goats NFTs act as a membership to the $GOAT DAO, the $GOAT Token acts as a governance token that ensures bigger holders have more decision power and can contribute more to the evolution of the ecosystem.

  • 5

    GoatSwap

    Goat Swap is the Mutant Goats exchange that will be deployed on the Ethereum Blockchain, and that will enable holders to stake their NFTs, provide liquidity, swap $GOAT for ETH and so much more.

  • 6

    Blue Chip Marketplace

    • Stake your Mutant Goats NFTs and earn daily $GOAT rewards. 
    • Exchange your $GOAT to blue chip NFT airdrop, (the community votes on what next blue chip NFT should be available on the GOAT marketplace).
  • 7

    Metaverse

    Metaverses offer new investment opportunities, and Lands are the perfect illustration of its potential, giving additional utility and value.

    As Metaverse lands are unpredictable territories, there has been a race to acquire land recently.

    Depending on the hype of the project and its success, we want to invest in a land with the community wallet, because we are attracted by the potential added value and return on investment of these lands. The holders will have a share in the value it gains in the future.
